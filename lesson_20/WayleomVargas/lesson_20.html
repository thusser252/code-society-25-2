My Favorite Manhwas

Here are a collection of my favorite murim genre manhwas

Legend of the Northern Blade

A revenge story about who saw his father death orchestrated by his closest confidants and the people he swore to protect. His father's mission was to protect the world from evil forces called the silent night. So Jin Mu-Won must take up his father's mantle as well as get revenge on those who killed him.

Gosu: The Master

Raised by his master for the purpose of revenge, Gang Ryong goes on a journey to eliminate his seniors who betrayed his master. He soon finds out that they have all have already passed. He then becomes a delivery boy for a restaurant but the world is much more dangerous than he realized.

Return of the Mount Hua Sect

After the devastating war with a group called the heavenly demonic cult, the Mount Hua sect was left to ruins after they sacrificed the majority of their members to defeat them. Cheonmyeong, the plum blossom swordsman, the one who struck the final blow to the leader of the cult, dies on the battlefield. 200 years later, he reincarnates with his memories intact and seeks to restore the Mount Hua sect to its former glory.
