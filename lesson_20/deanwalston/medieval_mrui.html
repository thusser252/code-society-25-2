Kingdoms

Powerful states ruled by a king or queen

  • territorial units that controlled land and resources
  • often engaged in wars with each other

Kings and Queens

Rulers of medieval kingdoms

  • hereditary ruler during their time in leadership
  • often married for political alliances
  • held absolute power in their realms

Knights

Brave warriors who fought for their lords

  • swore loyalty to their king or queen
  • followed a code of chivalry
  • often fought in battles on foot or horseback
